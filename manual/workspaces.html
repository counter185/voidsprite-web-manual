◆Workspaces

Every screen in voidsprite is a workspace. Think of them as separate tabs in a web browser - you can freely switch between them, close them and open new ones.

Your currently open workspaces are shown as squares in the bottom right corner of the window. The name of the currently active workspace is shown above them.
To view more info about a workspace, hover over it with the mouse pointer. You will see the name and a visual preview.

◆Switching workspaces

To switch to a different workspace, click its square in the bottom right corner. The currently active workspace will be highlighted.

You may also use the Previous workspace and Next workspace keybinds to switch between them (default: [ and ]).

Workspaces cannot be switched when a popup dialog is open.

◆Closing a workspace

To close a workspace, click the window's close button (the "X" in the title bar), or the Back button on Android.
Alternatively, use the File > Close option on the navigation bar.

◆Opening a new workspace

If you want to open an image in a new workspace, you can do so through the voidsprite Launchpad.
