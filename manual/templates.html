◆Templates

Templates are images that can be used as a starting point for new projects.
voidsprite comes with built-in templates, such as ones for RPG Maker 2000/2003 assets, but you can also add your own.

◆Custom templates

To add custom templates, put images in .voidsn or .png format in the templates/ folder in the Application data directory.

A good template should:

  • have the proper dimensions for the intended usecase
  • have a preset tile grid, if applicable
  • have comments explaining parts of the image.
